Pandesal with Egg

Ingredients: Pandesal, Egg, Oil

Steps:

  1. Fry Egg
  2. Slice Pandesal
  3. Place Egg
  4. Serve

Pancit canton with Egg

Ingredients: Pancit canton pack, Water

Steps:

  1. Boil Water
  2. Place pancit canton and egg
  3. Mix flavour packets in a plate
  4. Strain the water out the pancit canton and egg
  5. Mix with the pancit canton with the flavour packets
  6. Peel the egg
  7. Enjoy
