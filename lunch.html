Pork Giniling

Ingredients: Ground pork, diced potatoes, diced carrots, tomato sauce, garlic and onion, sugar, salt and pepper, sugar, cooking oil, water

Steps:

  1. Heat the cooking oil
  2. Put the garlic until it turns light brown
  3. Add onions until it becomes soft
  4. Put in the ground pork for about 5 minutes
  5. Add the carrots and potatoes then stir
  6. Simmer for 10 to 20 minutes
  7. Add salt,pepper. and sugar
  8. Enjoy!
