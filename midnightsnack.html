Midnight snack

Popcorn

Ingredients: Coconut oil, popcorn kernels, butter, salt

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil
  2. Place a few kernels into the oil
  3. Wait for them to pop
  4. Add the rest and cover the pot for 30 seconds
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and remove the kernels
  6. Melt butter into the empty pan and drizzle it all over the popcorn
  7. Sprinkle salt
  8. Enjoy!
