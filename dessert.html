Dessert

Banana Split

Ingredients: Banana, ice cream, chocolate syrup, whipped cream, strawberries or cherries

Steps:

  1. Peel the banana and cut it lengthwise
  2. Place scoops of ice cream in a row between the sliced banana
  3. Drizzle the chocolate syrup all over the ice cream
  4. Top each scoop of ice cream with whipped cream
  5. Add the strawberries / cherries
  6. Enjoy!
